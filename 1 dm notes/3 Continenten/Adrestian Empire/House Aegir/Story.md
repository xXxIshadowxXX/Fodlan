This house of the Imperial Prime Ministers, house Aegir often enjoys a similar amount of power to the Imperial household, if not more. This was especially true after the Insurrection of the Seven but has since fallen back to its former place after Emperor Edelgard took the throne. House Aegir is known for having the Crest of Cichol. The current head of the house is technically [Duke Ludwig von Aegir](https://fireemblem.fandom.com/wiki/Ludwig), however he has been on house arres, and his son [[Ferdinand von Aegir.webp]] has been managing the territory ever since.

Duke Ludwig von Aegir is a man deserving of death. He played no small part in the imprisonment of Edelgard and her siblings and the unimaginable torture they suffered while imprisoned. His continued position in power is but one of a string of constant reminders of her mutilation and humiliation. He even holds her father as a puppet emperor, who is absolutely destroyed at the guilt for what happened to his children. Execution would be a mercy for such a wretched man. And yet she merely puts him on house arrest when she is coronated as emperor.
