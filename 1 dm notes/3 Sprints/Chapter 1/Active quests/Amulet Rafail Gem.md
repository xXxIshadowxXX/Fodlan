### Het item:
Rafail Gem
 Wondrous Item, Hero's Relic, (Requires attunement by a
 creature with the Crest of Lamine).
 A protective necklace given to the witch Lamine, of the Ten
 Elites. It was in the care of House Martritz until the house
 fell. Now its whereabout are unknown, though the heir of
 Hrym seems to be seeking it for unknown reasons.
 This gem has a number of charges that can be used to cast
 spells, if you are unattuned, the gem has a maximum of 5
 charges, if you are attuned, that number goes up to 10, or 14
 if you possess a Major Crest of Lamine. The gem regains
 1d6+1 charges daily at dawn. If you cast a spell without any
 Crest, you suffer the Relic's Curse.
 Spells:
 1 Charge: cure wounds, shield of faith, protection from evil
 and good, detect magic, thunderwave.
 2 Charges: enhance ability, prayer of healing, protection
 from poison, healing word.
 4 Charges: beacon of hope, remove curse, daylight.
 5 Charges: revivify, mass healing word.
 Casting spells from the gem requires no material
 components. The gem has spell save DC of 15, a spell attack
 bonus of +7, and a spellcasting modifier of +4. If you wish, you
 can replace any of these with your own.
 Aigis: While attuned to this gem, all critical hits against
 you become regular hits, and you cannot be vulnerable to any
 damage.
