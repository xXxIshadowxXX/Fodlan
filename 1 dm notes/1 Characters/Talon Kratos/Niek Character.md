
### Organizations

### Allies

The Flickering Lanterns

A loose network of rebels, vagabond monks, and disenfranchised inventors who oppose secret cabals and abusive rulers.

They value stealth, knowledge, and guerrilla tactics.

They see you as a trusted weapon, but sometimes worry about your emotionless approach to messy human politics.

### Enemies

The Prime Cogs, leaders of the Clockwork Creed
The Iron Sovereign, grand leader of the clockwork creed

Secret inner council of the Clockwork Creed, believed by most to be myth.

Master manipulators who believe the world must be “maintained” through control, assassinations, and engineered "natural" disasters.

They view your defection as a critical flaw in the Creed’s programming — one that must be corrected or eliminated.

### Backstory

Forged over a century ago in the hidden Foundry of of the citystate, you awoke not into war or servitude, but into purpose. The Clockwork Creed, a reclusive order of monks and artificers, taught you to see the world as a grand machine — one whose gears needed careful tending. You trained in the silent art of precision: disabling tyrants, protecting the innocent from unseen threats, and preserving balance from the shadows.

Yet while your creators instilled you with discipline, your soul — yes, you have one — yearned for something more chaotic, more alive. Memories of a sweetheart from the Creed, a kitsune who dreamed of the stars beyond the monostery, stir in your mind. You chose a different path, leaving the cloistered walls behind to experience true freedom and justice firsthand.

You wander now, a ghost of metal and purpose, saving those who cannot save themselves. But always, you wrestle with an inner contradiction: you are a perfect machine… built to serve imperfection.
