
### Organization
#### **Kingdom of Avaloria**

- **Rulers**: King Sebastiaan (an elf) and Queen Merida (a human).
    
- **Population**: Mixed, including elves and humans.
    
- **Protection**: The kingdom is protected by a god named **Helm**, who safeguards the reigning king and queen from harm.
    
- **Royal Line**: Caroline Everly is the princess and heir to the throne.
    
- **Current Status**: Under threat from a werewolf faction seeking to overthrow the monarchy and destroy the population.
    

#### **Werewolf Tribe**

- **Leader(s)**: Ethan Thornheart’s father (unnamed) appears to be a key figure, likely the alpha or a high-ranking member.
    
- **Objective**: To overthrow Avaloria's rulers, kill the civilians, and create a new werewolf kingdom, empowered by marrying into the royal line to bypass Helm's divine protection.
    
- **Tactics**: Use deception, manipulation, and forced transformation. They turned Caroline into a werewolf against her will and tried to use her as a pawn.
### Allies
#### **Lilith**

- **Role**: Witch and protector of King Sebastiaan and Queen Merida.
    
- **Abilities**: Can sense magical changes (such as Caroline’s werewolf transformation), perform protective spells, and create magical items (like the ring that blocks Ethan’s control).
    
- **Actions**: Helped Caroline escape by giving her the magical ring and sacrificing herself to hold off pursuing werewolves.
    
- **Fate**: Presumed dead after being surrounded and attacked by werewolves during Caroline’s escape.
    

#### **Beauty**

- **Who**: Lilith’s horse.
    
- **Role**: Helped Caroline escape the werewolves and has stayed with her as her only companion in exile.
### Enemies
#### **Ethan Thornheart**

- **Role**: Initially Caroline’s royal guard and love interest; later revealed to be a werewolf and a traitor.
    
- **Motivation**: To gain power by marrying Caroline and becoming king of Avaloria, allowing the werewolf tribe to overthrow the monarchy.
    
- **Actions**: Deceived Caroline, transformed her into a werewolf, tried to control her as her alpha, and played a public role as her fiancé while privately ignoring and manipulating her.
    

#### **Ethan’s Father**

- **Role**: Leader or influential member of the werewolf tribe.
    
- **Actions**: Gave Ethan a necklace as part of the plan to bring Caroline to them; present at her kidnapping and transformation.
    

#### **Werewolf Tribe**

- **Description**: A hidden group of werewolves orchestrating the destruction of Avaloria.
    
- **Tactics**: Covert killings in the kingdom, forced transformation, manipulation through emotional attachment, and ritualistic or magical control of transformed werewolves.
