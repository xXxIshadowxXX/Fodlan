### Crest of Blaiddyd 
The crest of the Grim dragon. Once per attack, when you roll damage for a ranged magic attack and roll the maximum value for the one of the dice, you may roll the die an additional time and add both values to the damage. 
**Major Crest:** This increases to up to three times per attack. 
**Aspects:** Uncontrolled strength, Desire for justice. 
**Arcana:** VIII, Justice
