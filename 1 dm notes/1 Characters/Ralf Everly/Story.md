### Crest of Lamine
 The crest of the Aegis Dragon 
 When you cast a spell of 1st to 3rd level that restores hit points or cures ailments, roll a d6, on a 1, you don't lose the spell slot or use of the spell. on a 2 you can add 1d4 to healing. on a 3 you can add 1d6. on a 4 you can use healing word once as a reaction this day (not stackable).
 **Major Crest:** The ability works on spells up to 5th level, and once per long rest you can activate the ability when it would fail, but only on slots up to 3rd level. 
 **Aspects:** Kindness, Alchemically adept. 
 **Arcana:** XX, Judgement
 **spells**: gets healing word level 1
