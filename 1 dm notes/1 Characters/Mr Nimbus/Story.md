### Crest of Timotheos 
The crest of the Dark Dragon. When you cast a spell of 1st to 3rd level that gives positive effects other than restoring hit points, roll a d6, on a 1, you don't lose the spell slot. 
**Major Crest:** The ability works on spells up to 5th level, and once per long rest you can activate the ability when it would fail, but only on slots up to 3rd level. 
**Aspects:** Animal friendship, Beast warding. 
**Arcana:** XVII, The Star
