### Backstory
Jochem grew up in a lavish mansion, surrounded by treasures, books, and magical trinkets. His parents, brilliant historians and collectors, were protective, keeping him isolated from the world. He was content with knowledge, curiosity, and indulgence, until their passing left him with everything: wealth, responsibility, and the urge to explore. He left the mansion, eager to experience the world and its wonders.

When Jochem was around 30, he met **Alina**, a Far Traveler from the Vuelsa tribe. Her people lived in harmony with nature, connected to dreams and the spiritual realm, but Alina felt restricted by her tribe’s traditions. She left in search of something more. Their paths crossed in a village, where Jochem was struck by her wisdom and calm, while Alina saw past his wealth and status to the loneliness beneath.

Over time, they became close friends. Alina helped Jochem see the world beyond material wealth, teaching him the value of responsibility and thoughtful action. As Alina’s magical abilities grew, she discovered she was a wizard and began to develop her powers. She wasn’t just a friend—she became a guide, helping Jochem grow in ways he hadn’t expected.

Their bond deepened as they continued their travels. Jochem, once impulsive and driven by curiosity, began to understand that true adventure wasn’t just about collecting treasures. It was about growth, choices, and the people you meet along the way. Alina, still searching for her place in the world, found purpose in helping Jochem become the person he needed to be.
