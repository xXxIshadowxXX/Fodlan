Major Deities
- *Auril*: Goddess of winter.
- *Bane*: God of tyranny and fear.
- *Chauntea*: Goddess of agriculture.
- *Cyric*: God of lies, murder, and strife.
- *Helm*: God of protection.
- *Kelemvor*: God of the dead and the afterlife.
- *Lathander*: God of birth, renewal, and vitality.
- *Lolth*: Demon Queen of Spiders, the primary deity of the drow (dark elves).
- *Mystra*: Goddess of magic.
- *Oghma*: God of knowledge.
- *Selûne*: Goddess of the moon.
- *Shar*: Goddess of darkness and loss.
- *Silvanus*: God of wild nature.
- *Sune*: Goddess of love and beauty.
- *Tempus*: God of war.
- *Torm*: God of courage and self-sacrifice.
- *Tymora*: Goddess of good fortune.
- *Tyr*: God of justice.
- *Umberlee*: Goddess of the sea.
- *Waukeen*: Goddess of trade and wealth.

Evil Deities
- *Asmodeus*: Lord of the Nine Hells.
- *Gruumsh*: God of orcs.
- *Vecna*: God of secrets and undead.
- *Zehir*: God of poison and assassins.

Other Entities
- *Bahamut*: God of justice, protection, and nobility, often associated with metallic dragons.
- *Tiamat*: Goddess of greed and envy, often associated with chromatic dragons.
- *The Raven Queen*: Goddess of death, fate, and winter
