Dagda is not actually a nation, but rather an entire continent far to the west of Fodlan. There are no actual natives to Dagda, or if there were, they were long gone by the time settlers got there. The first recorded settlers of Dagda arrived around Imperial year 589, and progress in taming the wild continent has been extremely slow. Dagda's size puts Fodlan to shame, and some say it stretches from the southern tip of the world all the way to the north. This great size has confused explorers, who all come back with different accounts of the landscape based on where they landed.

### Culture 
Dagda has no consistent culture. Though there is one element that stays throughout most of the continent: sleep. Due to the dangers of the wild beasts that prowl many of the lands, it's always good to have a sizable group of people awake at all times. Dagdan daily life is centered around each person having their own sleep schedule, and most Dagdans have exceptional night vision as a result. 

### Ecology 
As you have heard before, there's no consistent ecology throughout Dagda, but a common theme is that of dangerous, unknown beasts, some of them quite massive.

### People of Dagda 
Having lived in a harsh environment, and with many being the descendants of less-than-friendly settlers, the people of Dagda are generally no-nonsense and to-the point. Dagdans are not untrustworthy however, and can usually be trusted to keep their promises, for better or worse.
