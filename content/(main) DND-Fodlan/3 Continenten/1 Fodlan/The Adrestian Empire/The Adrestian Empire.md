
The oldest of the four countries, the Adrestian Empire has slowly changed from the centerpoint of the church and into an anti-religious land of scholars.

### History of the Empire
#### Origins
The Empire was founded in Imperial Year 1, by Wilhelm I and Saint Seiros. It initially claimed dominion of Fodlan's southern half, but after the War of Heroes concluded the northern regions were taken as well.

#### The First Brigid and Dagda War
In Imperial Year 721, the allied forces of Brigid and Dagda attacked and annihilated the western Empire, as well as Bacchus, an allied neighbor. The Empire retaliated, and Brigid was subjugated as a vassal. The Empire attempted an attack on Dagda as well, but because of the great distance between the lands, the campaign was a failure. Brigid was eventually released from its bonds (only to be taken again in a few hundred years), but the land of Bacchus was so badly damaged that it was decided it would become a part of the Empire.

#### The War of the Eagle and Lion
In 747, the leader of house Blaiddyd, Loog, incited a revolt in the north to west corner of the Empire. In 751, the war ended with the Kingdom of Faerghus being officially recognized as an independent country. Later, in 801, the Leicester region also decided to revolt, and joined the Kingdom. Though inheritance disputes would later cause the region to rebel once more and become the Leicester Alliance.

#### The Southern Chruch
In 1065, a branch of the Church of Seiros, the Southern Church, formed an insurrection in Enbarr. It was put down as quickly as it started, and house Varley was formed as the Ministry of Religion to take the Southern Church's role. This would be the start of tense relations between the Church of Seiros and the Empire.

#### The second Brigid and Dagda War
In 1158, Brigid and Dagda once again attacked the Empire, this time landing in the territories of Menja and Enlupmoz. The invasion was suppressed, and Brigid was once more made into a vassal state, this time with no intention of release.

#### The insurrection of the Seven
In 1167, after realizing that Emperor Ionius IX was attempting to centralize power to the throne, house Hrym attempted to defect to the Leicester Alliance. House Hrym was completely wiped out by the Empire, with the whole genetic line being executed After this event the other noble families grew worried, and in 1171 Duke Ludwig von Aegir rallied all the other major nobles into rebellion. The Emperor was stripped of his power and made into a puppet leader, with the Duke Aegir becoming the effective head of the Empire.

#### Princess Edelgard
In 1180, the Princess Edelgard took control of the Empire from the hands of her father Ionius IX, who was grateful for the opportunity to hand it over, as he was old and sick. She then disposed of over half the nobles in power, either by assassination or house arrest.

### Aspects of the Empire
#### Architecture
Adrestian architecture takes inspiration from the Roman Empire. Aqueducts and stoneworking give larger cities a feeling of technological superiority compared to the rest of Fodlan

#### Respect and Nobility
Due to a prevalence of scholars and nobles, even the common people try to keep an aura of professionalism and respect. Outbursts of emotion are frowned upon, and art is most appreciated when it is minimalistic and functional.

#### Sages versus Scholars
While the Empire may now be a secular nation, it was still the center of religion for a long time. Nowadays those of faith face persecution, with some denoting them as "mindless drones" and others accusing them of plotting against the Empire. However, there is no official stance against any religion itself. In fact, the Empire has enlisted the help of the Western Church and house Varley in order to gather supporters from within the Church.

#### Old arts
Adrestian history dates back a long way. In major cities such as Enbarr, powerful organizations manage the nation's culture. Art is considered a posh and noble pursuit, and so the majority of art and music is done in traditionally highclass mediums. The Mittelfrank Opera House is one such example of these concepts.

### Noble Houses of the Empire
#### Hresvelg
House Hresvelg is the Imperial Family. By tradition, the Emperor takes multiple consorts in order to produce as many heirs as possible. The family received the blessing of Saint Seiros, and thus the family bears her Crest. After the Insurrection of The Seven, a suspicious string of misfortunes befell the Emperor's children, and only one survived: the now-Emperor Edelgard.

###### Locations in the Territory
 Actual governance of the region is done by house Vestra.
* **Enbarr** is the Imperial capital province, with sprawling waterways said to be created by Saint Seiros herself through miracles. The city is home to the Mittelfrank Opera Company, which is well-regarded as the greatest collection of bards in Fodlan.
* **Mavric** Is a large fishing city, which provides a port for those traveling to and from the island of Bacchus

#### Vestra
House Vestra serves as the Ministry of the Imperial Household, the family is responsible for assisting with the Emperor's responsibilities, such as preparing for rituals, taking care of the Imperial consorts and children, and maintaining the Imperial Guard. The family does not have any members with crests, as interweaving between other houses could cause a conflict of interests. The current Marquis is Hubert von Vestra, who took his father's position after he personally conducted the assassination. House Vestra has no territory of its own, but is in charge of governing the territories of house Hresvelg

#### Aegir
This house of the Imperial Prime Ministers, house Aegir often enjoys a similar amount of power to the Imperial household, if not more. This was especially true after the Insurrection of the Seven but has since fallen back to its former place after Emperor Edelgard took the throne. House Aegir is known for having the Crest of Cichol. The current head of the house is technically Duke Ludwig von Aegir, however he has been on house arres, and his son Ferdinand has been managing the territory ever since.

###### Locations in the Dukedom
The Dukedom of Aegir has expanded tremendously over the last few decades, after it has taken claim of lands whose noble families deteriorated
* **Boramas** is the capital city of house Aegir. It is a shining territory with wealth displayed around every corner. The city originally had problems with poor people being cast into the shadows and brought to starvation, but now the young lord has taken care that all peoples are given a chance to thrive in the domain.
* **Rusalka** is a small territory to the west of boramas, filled with lakes and a small village. The area is home to all sorts of wandering monsters, demonic or otherwise, making it a good place for hunters to find good (if dangerous) work. The area was originally ruled by house Martritz, but when the house was abolished it was taken in by Aegir, who imposed taxes on the land and otherwise let it run itself.

#### Bergliez
House Bergliez serves as the Ministry of Military Affairs. The family is well-respected for their might and strategy, though often criticized for their emotional outbursts. The family has the Crests of Cichol and Indech, but it's uncommon for a member to actually bear either of the crests. The family of Bergliez is quite large, with many members competing for influence. Despite this, they all seem to get along well, and they serve as a model for other noble families. The current head is General Leopold von Bergliez.

###### Locations in the County
The County of Bergliez encompasses the eastern plains of the Empire. Holding the largest territory out of all the families, though most of it remains empty and unused
* **Fort Merceus** is the largest citadel in the Empire, and bears the nickname "The Stubborn Old General". It, and the surrounding town, serve as the seat of house Bergliez. The area is known for its abundant agriculture and fishing
* **Gronder Field** is a large valley surrounded by small mountains. The various farms within produce much of the eastern Empire's food. It also serves as an easily defendable location, and was the original site of the decisive battle in the War of the Eagle and Lion. In recent years, it was used as the field of the mock battles held by the Officer's Academy.

#### Hevring
House Hevring serves as the Empire's Ministry of Internal Affairs, maintaining the judiciary, financing, and administration, among other tasks. They often clash with houses Bergliez and Varley. The family is descended from monks who worked alongside Saint Cethleann, and therefore bear her Crest. The current head of the house is Count Waldemar von Hevring.

###### Locations in the County
The territories of Hevring encompass the southern regions of the mountains known as Fodlan's Fangs, as well as the areas connecting the side of the empire with aveloria The area is warm and swampy due to water from the mountains, with plants that cannot be found anywhere else in Fodlan
* **Mozghuz** is house Hevring's main territory. The city has a prosperous mining business, gathering coal, silver, and copper from the Oghma Mountains.
* **Enlupmoz** a large port city settled to make the trip to Menja more easy and is the most swamped part of this territory.

#### Varley
House Varley serves as the Mistry of Religion, and maintains relations with the Church. However, with rising tensions they now focus on keeping the loyalty of believers in the Empire. They also have taken on some aspects of the judiciary, causing conflicts with house Hevring. The family holds the Crest of Indech. The current Count Varley, Grégoire von Varley, is placed under house arrest. His daughter has technically been granted his role temporarily, but she leaves the actual duties to her retainers

###### Locations in the County
The territory of Varley is small, only encompassing a small region below Garreg Mach. The area is surprisingly dry and arid, despite the proximity to various rivers. Territory disputes with Bergliez are common, due to their close proximity.
* **Varley** serves as house Varley's centerpoint, and gains most of its industry from mining adamantine and iron from the nearby mountains.

#### Hrym
The family of Hrym was executed after attempting to join the Alliance and causing a rebellion. The current Viscount Hrym is actually a general who was given both the title and name by Duke Aegir, and serves only as a puppet ruler. His adopted son, the young Lord Jeritza, serves in the Empire's army. This house bears no crest. 

###### Locations in the Viscounty
Hrym is a small city overridden with crime. Harsh taxes imposed onto the people have driven many to banditry. Many attempt to flee past the Airmid River and into Ordellia territory.
* **Hrym** is a small city overridden with crime. Harsh taxes imposed onto the people have driven many to banditry. Many attempt to flee past the Airmid River and into Ordellia territory, only to find similar circumstances awaiting them there.

#### Fenya
House Fenya is a minor house that controls territory in the southernmost ends of the Empire. They are a mostly peaceful sort, which has led to bandit troubles in recent years, as well as suspicions of allowing draft-dodging. The family of Fenya bears no crests.

###### Locations in the Viscounty
While Fenya started as a mining territory, the mines would eventually dry up. While this could have easily spelt doom for the region, good leadership allowed the region to switch its efforts to farming.
* **Fenya** Is a city that makes up the majority of infrastructure in Fenya The area had a rich gem mine, but when it was depleted centuries ago, the city was able to swap production to producing oils and wines. The only purpose Fenya's old mines now serve is to harbor bandits and foolhardy prospectors, both looking to earn quick money.

#### Menya
Distantly related to house Fenya, House Menya is a minor house that controls territory in the Westmost coasts of the Empire. The family of Menya bears no crests.

###### Locations in the Viscounty
The viscounty of Menya heavily relies on its coastal status and proximity to Brigid. It took a significant hit in the Second Brigid and Dagda War, but was able to recover due to trading with the new vasel nation.
* **Menya** Is a large city that makes up the majority of infrastructure in Menya. The city is known for its shipbuilding, and provides most of the ships used in the Imperial Navy.

### Abolished houses of the Empire
In recent years, political turmoil has left certain houses without the needed heirs, or with heirs unable or unwilling to take over the family.

#### House bartels
The family of Bartels had a history of shady dealings and immoral actions. They constantly sought out crest-bearing brides for their heirs, in order to add as many crests as possible to their bloodline. Due to their crest-obsessed nature, house Bartels had the Crests of Cichol, Cethleann, Indech, Charon, and, most recently, Lamine. After their mines depleted, the family became stuck in a power struggle. In 1176, most of the family was found dead in their home. The only survivor was the youngest son, Emile. The young man was thought by many to have murdered his family to win the power struggle, but after he left his inheritance behind, the rumors were seemingly proven false

#### House Martritz
House Martritz was a minor house with little influence, but a sizable amount of territory. The last baron Martritz had no sons, and his widow and daughter were prevented from inheriting the house by political schemes. The house was descended from Lamine, of the Ten Elites, and thus bear her Crest. The Hero's relic known as the Rafail Gem was owned by house Martritz, but its whereabouts are unknown.

###### Locations in the Barony
* **Rusalka** Is a small territory to the north east of the empire filled with mountains and a small village. The area does mostly fine without the noble house, though they no longer have any official protection from the bandits to the south. The people of the area are tough and capable, so they don't worry too much.
