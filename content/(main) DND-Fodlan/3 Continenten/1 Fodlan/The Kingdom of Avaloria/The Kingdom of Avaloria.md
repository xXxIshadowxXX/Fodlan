
### History of the Avalorian's
#### Origin
Following the Crescent Moon War there was not enough food to go around for a major part of the civilication. The eastern/western part of the kingdom which used to provide most of the food for the kingdom. blamed the northern part of the kingdom for having focused to much effort in the war while not having provided any manpower to the war themself. The avelorian commonwealth which was never loyal to the kingdom declared themself independend. The Church of seiros helped secure enough food for the Kingdom of Faerghus for the comming years from the new nation and the empire. This ensured no revolt would take place.

#### The 'ghost' king
In Imperial Year 721 When Dagda and brigid attacked the continent of fodlan, while mainly focusing on the empire at the start. the Kingdom of Aveloria got harshly attacked as well in a battle at the city of Nuvelle the kingsarmy won the battle but its king got kidnapped to Dagda. Because of the great distance between the nations Aveloria was unable to mount a full rescue operation. After a few years and brigid having been defeated the king got released and came back to Aveloria via a small fleet in return for some gold. Changed in mind and appearance the should be cheerfull nation was not sure if they got back the true king they were missing. The only proof was the birthmark of Everlyn being on the right place of his body.

#### The lost heirs
having their son abandon his claim to the throne the king and queen became obsessed with finding a marriage partner for their daughter to be able to secure the bloodline and keep the kingdom under their rule. in the Imperial Year 1176 having finnaly been able to convince their daughter their daughter to marry someone the Avelorian kingdom was in a cheerful mood. A few days before the marriage the princess disapeard and was nowhere to be found. The abdecated prins came back to the capitol searching despretly for his beloved sister. In the following years the prince looked for his sister in the regions surrounding the capitol. Comming back usually within the week. Now Imperial year 1180 the prince decided to look beyond and traveled around the continent. leaving the king and queen without heir.

#### Unusual animal attacks
The last few years the Avelorians have been plagued by animal attacks. Normal commoners having been bitten or left with scratched has become an almost common occurance. Convoys of nobles have also been known to have been attacked, Details of these attacks however ans usually kept secret. No scholor or researcher has been able to figure out what caused these animals to go rampant. 

#### The just king and queen
The king sebastion and queen Merida are known for their strict but just discipline on their children and territory, harsh punishments are not uncommen. However these harsher punishments would only come about if there had been given enough research and proof in these cases. Trying to manage their kingdom themself as much as possible their children went without attention and it is known that their children was raised by a caretaker.

### Aspects of the Avalorian's
#### Architecture
The architecture of this kingdom evokes a medieval fairy-tale atmosphere, with castles and manor houses built from sturdy natural stone, surrounded by lush gardens and wildflowers. Narrow cobblestone streets and wooden half-timbered houses with steep roofs define the small villages, often nestled among dense forests/mountains or along gently flowing rivers.

#### Equality for us
Man and women are (almost) equal in this country, this is thown in a long line of female queen succesors even when there were male heirs. Looking down upon others as lower forms of life is normal in this kingdom. There are some rare people who don't share this vieuw.

#### The absolute King
The everly bloodline was always sure of having enough heirs for the throne that it saw no need for ranks between nobles, they were to all anwer to them directly and no-one should be able to get a shot at the throne. This was a more profitable but stressfull way of rule and made all other nobles equal to each other.

#### Partanimals
Using whatever excuse possible avelorian's try and find the good off life. hosting large parties not only for noble but also for and by commoners. it is not unusual to hear one person celebrating their birthday and having the whole join them on a saturday.
When the parties are over for most commoners the grimness of reality sets back in and leave the happy party full of saddness.

### Noble Houses of the Avalorian's
#### House Everly
House Avaloria, the ruling family of the kingdom, descends from the Lamine bloodline. Known for their unwavering belief in the divine right of kings, they have ruled with absolute authority for centuries, ensuring that no noble claims can threaten their throne. Though the kingdom’s past is marked by turmoil—lost heirs, mysterious attacks, and political unrest—the Avalorians maintain order through strict discipline and a just, if stern, rule. through their uncommon traditions which sees male and female as equal the name of this house has changed multiple time,s from Lamine to Avaloria to currently Everly

###### Locations in the Dukedom
The dukedom of Avaloria covers a large area on the border of [[The Kingdom of Faerghus]]. Not recognized by Faerghus this dukedom laid claim to the whole Claw of Fodlan mountain region.
* **Avaloria** is a Magical city that served as Avaloria's main line of defense. It also serves as house Everly's seat of power. The city has received huge boons in trade from the [[The Kingdom of Faerghus]] in recent years.

#### House Arundel
A previously small house that rose to much greater power after one of their daughters was taken in as the queen consort of the empire. The house is currently ruled by her brother, Lord Volkhard von Arundel. The house bears no crest in the bloodline.

###### Locations in the Territory
The territories of Arundel encompass the largest part of the broder with the church of seiros
* **Arundel** is the primary domain of the house and is a wellfortified military city. The city is known as a center of faith in the Kingdom of Avaloria.

#### Ochs
House Ochs serves to regulate trade between Brigid and the empire. The house has struggled greatly in the past few years because of new trade routes being made. The house members occasionally bear the Crest of Macuil, but such occurrences are rare. The house has lost several Barons in the past few years. One Baron Ochs was lost in the helping of the second Brigid and Dadga war. Another, the former Baron's brother, was turned into a demonic beast after a black-market-deal gone wrong. The house is now led by the latter baron's widow

###### Locations in the Territory
* **Ochs** is a small town that gains its industry from trade and fishing

#### Gerth
While originally a minor noble family who occupied territory on the Brionac Plateau, they took their current position after house Nuvelle was wiped out. They're kept quite busy during the war. Despite the circumstances, Duke Gerth has been working with the last surviving member of house Nuvelle to restore her former power without having to sacrifice house Gerth's influence. The family of Gerth bears no crests.

###### Locations in the Territory
* **Gerth** is a city in the lower areas of the Brionac Plateau. The area's main source of income is woodcutting and gathering from the Plateau. 
* **Nuvelle** is a small city that previously belonged to the house of the same name.

#### Essar
House Essar is a house that is currently trying to establish themselves as a major pillar of the Kingdom of Avaloria. Specifically, they wish to be the center of education and research. Their plans have been set back by a notable heir abandoning his inheritance to pursue his own research at Garreg Mach. House Essar bears the Crest of Indech.

###### Locations in the Territory
Nestled just above the Hevring Swamps, Essar is situated at a key point to officiate trade.
* **Lupin** Is the head of Essar's power. The city is home to a number of research stations, and is second only to the church as the seat of scholarship in Fodlan. Besides these facilities, the economy also centers on foraging the rare plants and animals found in the surrounding Hevring swamps.

#### House Rowe
house Rowe is regarded with mixed renown, some believe them to be the new standard for knighthood and nobility, and others believe them to be nothing more than scoundrels. Whatever the case, house Rowe has been gaining power quickly. House Rowe has taken in many spouses and adoptees who bear Crests, but so far no child has yet been born into the family that bears one.

###### Locations in the Territory
The county of Rowe covers a large area in the north of Avaloria
* **Rindeston** is the base of operations for the Western Church. 
#### House Gaspard
In 1180, Lord Lonato of Gaspard assisted in the rebellion of the Western Church. He was killed in battle, but his actions were known around the continent. Now, his adoptive son Ashe is set to inherit the lands, along with his siblings. The Gaspard line has been wiped out, but their legacy continues. Neither bloodline ever contained a Crest.

###### Locations in the Territory
Gaspard territory lies south of Rowe, and next to the Church of Seiros and the [[The Kingdom of Faerghus]] border.
* **Gaspard** is the main town within the territory. The quality of life has decreased recently, as many of those capable of fighting have been sent to aid in the war efforts. 
* **Magdred Way** is a forest path connecting castle Gaspard to Garreg Mach. The area is known for its scenic views, but can often find itself enshrouded in fog some times of the year.

##### House Duval
House Duval occupies a large but undeveloped region of norther Avaloria. While the region has little in the way of natural resources, it's home to many different sacred and historical sites. House Duval has no known Crests.

###### Locations in the Territory
The county of Duval contains a number of sacred sites, which primes the region to focus its economy on accommodating pilgrims.
* **Duval** is a small city known for its hospitality. It's wellliked as a spot for nobles to experience the Kingdom's colder climate without the hazards of a full Faerghus winter that come from northern territories. 
* **Lake Teutates** is a huge lake to north of the City of Duval. In the dry seasons, the water recedes enough to reveal an ancient temple beneath the water. It's also said that a giant monster lurks beneath the waters, but no such rumors have been proven.

### Abolished houses of the Avalorian's
In recent years, political turmoil has left certain houses without the needed heirs, or with heirs unable or unwilling to take over the family.

#### House Nuvelle
This house served as the previous Ministry of Foreign Affairs. They benefited greatly from (mostly food) trade with other nations, and were one of the most important houses in the Avaloria. Officially, the house bore the Crest of Macui. The house fell in 1175 to a distraction attack frmo the forces of Brigid and Almyra. The entire family was wiped out, with the exception of the second child and first daughter, Constance. The young heiress now works to reclaim her former house's power

###### Locations in the Territory
Nuvelle once occupied the far west portion of the Empire.
* **Nuvelle** is a small city that was the seat of Nuvelle's power. Now it has found itself in the domain of house Gerth. The city has mostly recovered from the invasion, but the scars can still be seen in some parts of the city.
