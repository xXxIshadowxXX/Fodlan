
### History of the church
The church's history is equal parts historical and mythological, as such, take what you read with a grain of salt.

#### Arrival of the Gods
In the beginning, the gods descended from their homes in the stars, bringing gifts to the people of Fodlan. they granted the champion Nemesis and Ten of his allies the power of their Crests, and made them the Hero's relics. They were tasked with keeping peace throughout the lands. However, Nemesis and his Elites would not stay loyal, when the gods went back to rest, they began a reign of tyranny which was mostly influenced by the other more evil gods. The people of Fodlan cried out for help, and the gods answered...

#### Seiros and the Four Saints
In the city of Enbarr, five figures emerged, most prominent among them being the Saint Seiros herself. Like the Ten Elites, they were granted the power of Crests, but unlike the Elites, they were not granted Hero's Relics, but instead the ability to share their Crests with those whom they trusted most. The Saint Seiros partnered with Wilhelm I to create the Adrestian Empire, as well as the Imperial Calendar. They then set off to war against nemesis. Each of the four Saints; Cichol, Cethlean, Indech, and Macuil, helped in their own ways, and granted Crests to their loyal assistants. Eventually the war was won, and the Church of Seiros brought peace to Fodlan.

#### Completion of Garreg Mach
In 185, [[Garreg mach monestery]] was officially completed. This wonder of architecture would serve as the headquarters of the Church of Seiros, and remains the most impressive structure in Fodlan to this day.

#### The Officer's Academy
In 981, in response to growing conflicts with Almyra, the church took it upon itself to raise the nobility of Fodlan to ensure strong future leadership. The Officer's Academy was created to serve this purpose, and ten years later began accepting commoners who could pay the tuition fees

#### The Fire of 1159
At the end of the year 1159, a great fire spread throughout Garreg Mach. There was significant property damage, but the only casualties were the Captain Jeralt and his young child. Twenty years later, however, Jeralt was found to be alive, and had an adult child with him that he claimed was born after he left the monastery, obvious rumors abounded, but nothing substantial came of them

#### Western Rebellion
In early 1180, Lord Lonato Gaspard allied with the Western Church in a rebellion against the Central Church. While this rebellion ultimately failed, it paved the way for the Empire's war later the same year.

### Aspects of the Church
With the Church being as old and as powerful as it is, it's no wonder that it has its own share of culture and tradition.

#### Worship of the Gods
While the church is named after saint Seiros, their focus of worship is on the good [[Goden]] of Fodlan: Helm, Lathander, Mystra, Oghma, Selûne, Tymora, Tyr, Umberlee, and Waukeen. The Gods are seen as being omnipresent, but lacking a physical form on earth. It is believed that the spirits of the gods' followers will ascend into the sky after death, to join the gods in the afterlife. Each god has their respective representation in the chruch, the nature of the gods being so close together means there is almost no disagreement in actions and there is even a singular archbischop to act as a sort of pope. Even praying for diffrent gods is usually done together and most folowers of the church pray to multiple if not all of the gods.

#### Peace above all else
In the Church of Seiros, conflict is highly despised. While the Church does need to occasionally employ military force, they do so swiftly and efficiently to reduce civilian casualties. Everything they do is to ensure a lasting peace, even if that requires taking away certain freedoms. Even on an individual scale, believers are asked to put aside their grievances

#### Seclusion and Reservation
The Church of Seiros encourages looking inward, and leaving other nations to their own devices. Open relations with other nations are discouraged, though not outright banned. The Church also states that uncontrolled technological progress would lead to disaster. As such, many technologies are banned across Fodlan. Occasionally, a technology is approved for re-examination, and sometimes a ban or two is lifted. Dissenters of the Church state that these rules are meant to prevent the people of Fodlan from reaching their true potential, while supporters argue that these are needed to keep peace between the nations so that none may overpower the others.

#### The Other Branches
While the **Central Church** is what most people think of first regarding the Church of Seiros, there are three other braches as well. 
* The **Western Church** holds more extreme beliefs than the others. They believe in greater separation of class and race, as well as put more emphasis on worshiping the Four Saints, especially Cichol. Residing in what is the north side of [[The Kingdom of Avaloria]].. 
* The **Eastern Church** is far more easygoing, due to being located within the Alliance. Weak and complacent.
* **The Southern Church** was wiped out after trying to take back the Empire in the name of Saint Seiros, with no retaliation from the other branches, who condemned their actions.

### Notable Figures of the Church
#### Saint Serios
The secondary focus of worship for the church, as well as its namesake. Saint Seiros revitalized worship of the Gods, after faith had been dwindling. She appeared in the town of Enbarr, and assisted the first Adrestian Emperor in the creation of his Empire. Seiros was said to have personally struck down Nemesis at the Tailtean plains, after which she oversaw the creation of Garreg Mach. She was laid to rest in the holy mausoleum, but an incident during the rebellion of the Western Church revealed that the body was missing. Seiros' Crest is not very rare, with many high-ranking members of the church possessing it

#### The Four Saints
The allies of Saint Seiros, these warriors fought against Nemesis and the Ten Elites, each with their own small base of followers. They were said to have lived extraordinarily long lives and granted their Crests to their followers. 
* **Saint Macuil** was an accomplished battlemage, proficient in sword and spell. He served as the tactician for the early church, as well as the blacksmith for many of the sacred weapons used in the war. He only gave his Crest to a select few individuals. 
* **Saint Indech** was a warrior of great strength and wisdom. Little is known about this saint, but it is said he was a great craftsman. He gave his Crest to the smiths and workmen he admired. 
* **Saint Cichol** was a formidable warrior and commander, as well as the father of Saint Cethleann. He was said to be a kind but stern authority, and is one of the most commonly revered of the Saints. He gave his Crest to some of his lieutenants. 
* **Saint Cethleann** was an empathetic healer and the daughter of Saint Cichol. While the subject of many fables, very little historical information regarding her life exists. She gave her Crest to some of the medics who assisted her on the field

#### Nemesis and the Ten Elites
While not worshiped, Nemesis and his Elites were still the chosen heroes of the Goddess, and still are respected. 
* **Blaiddyd:** A mage knight who wielded the now-lost magic of necromancy, as well as the Hero's Relic Areadbhar. 
* **Riegan:** An archer who wielded the relic Failnaught. It's said that saint Macuil convinced Riegan to sell information on Nemesis' forces
* **Lamine:** A witch who defected to the Church for unknown reasons. Her relic was the Rafail Gem. 
* **Goneril:** A violent and treacherous general of Nemesis' army. He wielded the relic Freikugel. 
* **Charon:** The pupil of Blaiddyd and master of blade and magic. He wielded the Relic Thunderbrand. 
* **Fraldarius:** A pegasus rider and the scout for Nemesis' armies. She used the Relic known as the Aegis Shield to protect herself on her missions
* **Gloucester:** A priest who began to worship an unknown god after his fall from grace. His son defected to the Church after he saw what his father was doing. He used the Hero's Relic Thyrsus. 
* **Dominic:** A mighty tactician who was no stranger to battle. He rode atop a wyvern and used the Relic known as Crusher. 
* **Gautier:** An armored general who was immovable on the battlefield. He used the Lance of Ruin as his Relic of choice. 
* **Daphnel:** A paladin who served Riegan as his right-hand man. He used the Relic Lúin
* **Maurice:** a swordmaster and secret eleventh Elite. His misuse of his Relic Blutgang led to him becoming a demonic beast. To prevent knowledge of such events, the church erased him from history. 
* **Nemesis:** Also known as "The King of Liberation". He was the only person in history to possess the "Crest of Flames". He used the Sword of the Creator to strike down entire armies at once

#### The Archbishop
The Archbishop is the highest position of the Church of Seiros. The title has been held by the descendants of Seiros herself. Each Bishop reigns until death, which can be over a century for some. The daughter of the Bishop is then given the position as her birthright. Strangely, no daughter of the Archbishop has ever been seen, as they are a closely guarded secret. Even stranger, no Archbishop has ever been confirmed to be pregnant. many wild conspiracy theories have arisen, but nothing has ever held ground. The current Archbishop is the lady Rhea.

#### The Cardinals
The Cardinals are the heads of the Church, after the Archbishop. They are chosen by the Archbishop, and their identities are a secret. They pose as ordinary monks or knights to protect their identities. Each Cardinal has their own specialty, usually an element of magic, that they have mastered
###### Aelfric
A former Cardinal, Aelfric Dahlman, was involved in a scandal in early 1180. Aelfric, who served as the Cardinal of Fire, had previously been known for his involvement of the renovations of Abyss. He was the one who put the current leader, Yuri, in power as well as formed the Ashen Wolves in order to give promising youths of Abyss the education of the Officer's academy. Aelfric used his authority in Abyss to retrieve the body of a nun, Sitri, and an artifact known as the Chalice of Beginnings. He attempted to use the relic to revive Sitri, but ended up transforming into a hideous beast and had to be put down. After this incident, the people of Abyss had to contend with new opposition, as there was one less figure to defend them.

#### The four apostels
These four figures were tasked with creating a new body for the goddess. They failed in their task, and decided to go into self-exile, while asking the Church to remove their names from history. Even basic knowledge of the Apostles is limited to high-ranking members of the Church. Like the saints, they possessed their own Crests, and could grant them to others. 
* Aubon retreated to Abyss, but eventually left to wander Fodlan. 
* Timotheos retreated to the forests of the old Adrestian Empire, where he founded the village that shares his name. 
* Noa retreated to the shores north of Fodlan's Fangs, where she created house Nuvelle alongside her adopted children. 
* Chevalier retreated to the Eastern mountains, and created the village of Kupala.
