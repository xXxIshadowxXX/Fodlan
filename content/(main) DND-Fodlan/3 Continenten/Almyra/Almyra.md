
### Almyra 
Almyra is a massive nation to the east of Fodlan. Almyra's size is slightly larger than Fodlan in its entirety. Almyra has a king that rules over the entirety of the land, but in order to manage the territories, various regents control different subterritories on the king's behalf.

### Culture 
Almryan culture has Persia and other middle-eastern cultures as its real-world inspiration. Almyra is an advanced warrior culture, and due to the nation's size, varies greatly between the various lands. Weapons are a large part of Almyran society, and many warriors carry a signature weapon from adulthood to death. In a similar vein, Almyrans have a great attachment to their mounts, and the death of a mount calls for the same level of grief as the loss of a comrade. Aside from militaristic culture, Almyrans are known for their knowledge of the sciences, namely alchemy, astronomy, and mathematics. They also have advanced agricultural techniques that the people of Fodlan have yet to understand. Corruption and conspiracy among nobility of Almyra is common. The king hold comparably little power compared to the royalty of Fodlan, and is helpless to prevent infighting within his nation.

### Ecology and Technology
Being as big as it is, Almyra cannot be easily summed up, and only the Western regions have been charted by Fodlan mapmakers. The Western regions of Almyra are dry and arid, with great, expansive plains and towering mesas, as well as the great redwoods to the north.

### Economy and technology
Almyra is just as advanced as Fodlan in most areas, with the exception of arcane magic. Almyra also exceeds Fodlan in many areas, including the aforementioned fields of alchemy, astronomy, and mathematics. Almyran weaponry is quite advanced, especially their bowcraft. In addition, the modern Riding Wyverns hailed from Almyra, and have been exported to all over the world. These two combined have caused a tradition of wyvern-riding archers called Barbarossas. Almyran commerce has suffered due to their reputation as warmongers, but merchants can still find ample opportunities if they are willing to persevere through the discrimination.

### People of Almyra
Almyran people are as varied as the landscape. Many common folk serve as warriors, farmers, and craftsmen, while those of noble birth are scientists and scholars. While corruption runs deep in Almyra, there are those who seek to oppose it. The crown prince, Khalid, who faced persecution and scrutiny due to his half-Fodlan birth, has left the country to gather allies to assist his future rule. In addition, the great general "Nader the Undefeated" has been attempting to cool aggressions at the Fodlan border to make negotiations easier.
