A frozen wasteland northwest of Fodlan. Albinea is mostly empty, save for a few small settlements. 

### Culture 
Albinea isn't much of a nation, more of just a few isolated settlements on a shared landmass. Each has their own customs and traditions. 

### Ecology 
Albinea is far too cold for agriculture, so inhabitants have to resort to hunting and fishing. 

### Economy and Technology 
Abinea uses basic technology that can be found in most small towns. The people are efficient and waste nothing. Albinea has no major exports, save a few kinds of meat from the wildlife. 

### People of Albinea 
The people of Albinea mostly come from Fodlan, having left for one reason or another. Due to their isolated location, they don't get much contact with the main continent.
